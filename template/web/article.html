Welcome to Lumbini Palace Resort

A Sanctuary of Nature & Luxury in Lumbini

Set in a water-world ambiance on 18 bigha of picturesque land in Siyari, Rupandehi, Lumbini Palace Resort blends with nature in the most sustainable way — without compromising modern-day luxury. Wander through ponds, explore organic farming, or unwind on our deck with breathtaking views of the sacred Lumbini plains.
